Hearts of Iron IV DLC Expansions

Hearts of Iron IV has several DLC expansions that add new features, countries, and scenarios to the game. Each DLC offers a unique gameplay experience and expands on different aspects of World War II and its aftermath. Here is a brief overview of each of the DLCs:

Together for Victory

Together for Victory is the first major expansion for Hearts of Iron IV. It focuses on the British Commonwealth and introduces new mechanics for puppet states and autonomous regions. The DLC adds new focus trees for Canada, Australia, New Zealand, India, and South Africa, allowing players to experience the war from the perspective of these major British allies. Together for Victory also includes new equipment and unit models for these countries, as well as new music and voices.

Death or Dishonor

Death or Dishonor is a DLC that focuses on the nations of Europe caught between the Axis and Allied powers. It adds new focus trees for Hungary, Romania, Czechoslovakia, and Yugoslavia, as well as new national events and decisions. The DLC also introduces a new "equipment conversion" feature, allowing players to convert outdated equipment to more advanced models. Death or Dishonor includes new music and voices for the featured countries, as well as new unit models and historical events.

Waking the Tiger

Waking the Tiger is a DLC that expands on the East Asian theater of World War II. It introduces new mechanics for generals and officers, allowing players to customize their military leadership. The DLC also adds new focus trees for China, Japan, and the warlords of China, as well as new national events and decisions. Waking the Tiger includes new music and voices for the featured countries, as well as new unit models and historical events.

Man the Guns

Man the Guns is a DLC that focuses on naval warfare and the major naval powers of World War II. It introduces new mechanics for ship design and modernization, as well as new technologies and research options. The DLC also adds new focus trees for the United States, United Kingdom, Mexico, and the Netherlands, allowing players to experience the war from a naval perspective. Man the Guns includes new music and voices for the featured countries, as well as new unit models and historical events.

La Resistance

La Resistance is a DLC that adds new mechanics for espionage and resistance movements. It introduces new focus trees for Spain and Portugal, as well as new national events and decisions related to intelligence and sabotage. The DLC also includes new music and voices for the featured countries, as well as new unit models and historical events.

Battle for the Bosporus

Battle for the Bosporus is a DLC that focuses on the nations of the Balkans and the Middle East. It adds new focus trees for Greece, Turkey, and Bulgaria, as well as new national events and decisions. The DLC also introduces new unit models and historical events related to the region, as well as new music and voices for the featured countries.

Together for Victory II

Together for Victory II is a DLC that expands on the British Commonwealth and introduces new mechanics for the Indian subcontinent. It adds new focus trees for India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh, as well as new national events and decisions. The DLC also includes new unit models and historical events related to the region, as well as new music
